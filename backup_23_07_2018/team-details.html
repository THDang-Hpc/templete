POSITION

Khandoker M. S. Kawsar is the Co-founder, CEO and Managing Director of the firm and specially looking after the International trade and Investment issues of the firm.


PROFESSIONAL AND ACADEMIC DETAILS

He is a Barrister-at-Law from Hon’ble Society of Lincoln’s Inn and did his BPTC (PgDL) from City Law School, City University London and LLB (Hon’s) under University of London. He also graduated in English Literature. He is practicing regularly in the Supreme Court of Bangladesh. He was an Associate to legendary lawyer Mr. Mahmudul Islam who was a Senior Advocate, Former Attorney General and also the author of ‘Code of Civil Procedure’ and ‘Constitutional Law of Bangladesh’. Besides, he is a Senior Faculty Member (Part Time) of Department of Law of Bhuiyan Academy (Providing external study of University of London). He was a Senior Lecturer of the Department of Law of Northern University Bangladesh (NUB) and was a Lecturer (Part time) of the Department of Legal studies as well as Student Welfare officer (Part time) of London Ambassador College (LAC), London, UK.


EXPERIENCE IN THE FIELD OF ARBITRATION

He has participated as Arbitration Counsel in a number of International Arbitrations and Arbitrator in several local Arbitrations held in Bangladesh. He is an Associate Member of Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (ACIArb) and a ‘Trainer’ in ‘Bangladesh International Arbitration Centre (BIAC)’, the only International Arbitration Institute in Bangladesh. He is also member of international organizations relating to Arbitrations- ICC YAF, YAIG, LCIA, YSIAC, SIAC. He undertook Advanced Training in Arbitration (ATA) and Training of the Trainers (ToT) jointly organized by BIAC and International Law Institution (ILI), Washington DC. Besides his providing of other trainings on behalf of BIAC, he was the key person of the delegates to provide training on ‘Practice and Art of Negotiation’ to 22 senior most officials of Karnaphuli Fertilizers Company LTD (KAFCO), the biggest international Joint Venture in Bangladesh on March 12-13 of 2017. He has participated in several seminars and workshops on Arbitration and Mediation, including, conference in International Arbitration by International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) and ICC International Court of Arbitration (ICA) in Hotel Grand Hayatt Regency, Delhi on 14th October 2017 and SIAC India Conference and Hard talk in Imperial Hotel New Delhi on 12th October as well as attended 'YSIAC Oral Advocacy Workshop' in Imperial Hotel of New Delhi on 13th october 2017. He attended BIAC's 6th Anniversary Seminar on 'Doing Business Index: ADR in Effective Enforcement of Contracts' on 07th October 2017, Seminar on "ADR: International best practice & success factors" organized by BIAC in DCCI Building dated 17th December 2016 and other one on ‘Workshop on Commercial Mediation’ organized by BIAC and DCCI dated 11th March 2016.


ATTACHMENT WITH TRADE AND INVESTMENT CONSULTANCY

He is a Member of the ‘Trade and Investment Working Committee’ of BUILD (http://www.buildbd.org/), a Dialogue, Advocacy and Recommendation platform for eradicating investment constraints in Bangladesh. He provides consultancy to some national and International organizations in International Trade and Investment area. He successfully gave some policy recommendations in the Dialogue Workshop named ‘Advanced Ruling-process for simplifying Import and Export Trade Procedures’ held on 15.11.2017 jointly organized by BUILD and Bangladesh Trade Facilitation Activity (BTFA)-USAID. He was one of the four presenters presenting in the issues ‘Anti-dumping, Countervailing duties and para-tariffs’ in a two day workshop organized by USAID and BUILD (Business Initiative Leading Development) on the topic ‘IPEC Bangladesh Non-Tariff Barriers Action planning Workshop’ held on 7th and 8th December 2016 where high profile national and international public and private delegates were present. He attended in the ‘Asia-Pacific Business Forum’ organized by ICC Bangladesh, Ministry of Commerce of Bangladesh and United Nations ESCAP in Pan Pacific Sonargaon Hotel, Dhaka, Bangladesh on 8-9th February 2017 and also was an invited guest in the ‘Investment and Policy Summit’ organized by Board of Investment of Bangladesh, DCCI, BUILD in Hotel Radisson, Dhaka on 24-25 January 2016 . He was an active discussant and contributor in the ‘Focus Group Discussion’ of Textile Sustainability Platform (TSP) of PaCT held on 1 August 2017 in BGMEA Bhaban, Dhaka.


OTHER ACTIVITIES

Mr. Kawsar presented the Key Note in the seminar entitled “Media laws of Bangladesh: Problems and Prospects” arranged by the Northern University of Bangladesh (NUB) in Hotel pan Pacific Sonargaon on 26th February 2011. As a Member of the Editorial Board, Barrister Kawsar is one of the active members of an International Journal on Law, Human rights and Social Science (a Peer Review Journal). Additionally he had surfing in Constitutional Law of Bangladesh, UK and USA. He has initiated to form the ‘Foundation for the Constitution Center’ (FCC) and under its banner ‘The National Constitution Center’ (NCC). He has undertaken an extensive research for a publication on “Sustainable provisions of the Constitution of Bangladesh attained from the comparative study of the constitutions of some countries of the world”. He has been teaching Constitutional Law of UK as well as of USA for several years. Besides, he has been practicing for several years as well as has been writing a book on the ‘VAT Act and Rules of 1991’ and doing researches on ‘VAT Act and Rules of 2012’. He has been attending in a number of TV Talk shows in Budget of Bangladesh, specially in Fiscal Law and the recent one for five consecutive days in Desh TV in the issue ‘Providing Legal assistance to SME sectors of Bangladesh’ as well as in ‘Financial Crime’. He has a number of writings published in the newspapers both in Bangladesh and in UK. He has experience of being Judges in the Moot Court Competitions held in Bhuiyan Academy for several times. He is very much interested to further his career in the sector of International Trade and Investment and in International Arbitration.
